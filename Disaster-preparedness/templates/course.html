Types of Earthquakes

  • Tectonic Earthquakes – caused by movement of plates
  • Volcanic Earthquakes – linked with volcanic activity
  • Collapse Earthquakes – caused by underground collapses
  • Explosion Earthquakes – caused by man-made explosions

How to Escape in an Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On
  • Stay indoors until shaking stops
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects
  • If outside, move to an open area away from buildings
